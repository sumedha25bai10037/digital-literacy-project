Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Do not click on suspicious links or emails
- Always check website URLs carefully
- Never share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone
- Verify UPI payment requests before accepting
- Use official apps for online transactions
- Keep your devices updated with security patches
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Report cybercrime on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
