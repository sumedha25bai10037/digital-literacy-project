I used Canva to create my Digital Literacy Awareness infographic because it is simple and user-friendly. My design explains the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. I included examples like Google Docs, Zoom, and Canva to make it relatable for students. I also added points about protecting personal information and using strong passwords to promote online safety.

While creating this infographic, I found it interesting to organize information in a visual way so that it is easy to understand. The most difficult part was selecting the right design and keeping the content short but meaningful. This task helped me understand the importance of digital literacy in student life.
